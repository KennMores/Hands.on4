About Us

Anson's PH - Our mission is to offer premium products and service at an affordable price, with a focus on customer value and exceptional service.

We’re a trusted source of premium appliances and electronics in the Philippines. With branches conveniently located throughout Metro Manila, we’re committed to providing our customers with exceptional products and services..

We believe in the importance of having a wide variety of options, which is why we offer a comprehensive range of product categories and brands to meet all of your appliance and electronics needs. Our dedicated staff is knowledgeable, attentive, and passionate about providing personalized attention and hassle-free service.

We are incredibly grateful for the opportunity to have served the community for over five decades. It’s an honor we take to heart. We remain committed to constantly growing and evolving to better serve our customers’ changing needs with humility and dedication.

At our company, Speed, Simple, and Service are the core values that drive us forward. While we’re proud of the progress we’ve made in incorporating these values into our operations, we understand that there’s always room for improvement. We’re constantly striving to grow and evolve, always looking for new and innovative ways to provide the best customer experience possible.

By focusing on these values, we’re able to provide a customer experience that’s truly unique and unparalleled. Whether you’re a long-time customer or just discovering us for the first time, we’re confident that you’ll appreciate our commitment to Speed, Simplicity, and Service, and our ongoing efforts to improve and grow.
